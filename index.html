Interesting Facts about Camels

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet
  2. Pigeons can move their wings ten times per second and maintain heartbeats at the rate of 600 times per minute
  3. Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour
